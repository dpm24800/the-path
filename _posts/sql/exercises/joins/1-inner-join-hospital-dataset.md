## **1 INNER JOIN Questions**
### **Basic (5)**
1. List all appointments with patient names and doctor names.
2. Find all prescriptions along with the corresponding appointment date and patient name.
3. Get all patients who have appointments with doctors in the “Cardiology” department.
4. List all completed appointments with patient and doctor details.
5. Show all prescriptions along with doctor names who prescribed them.

### **Intermediate (5)**
6. Find all patients and the number of appointments they have with doctors.
7. List all patients along with the specialties of the doctors they have seen.
8. Retrieve all appointments with patient name, doctor name, and medicine prescribed (if any).
9. Show patients and their last appointment date with a doctor.
10. List patients and all medicines prescribed to them, ordered by patient name.

### **Advanced (5)**
11. Find the doctors who have prescribed more than 3 different medicines to patients.
12. List patients who have seen more than 2 doctors along with the doctors’ names.
13. Find the total number of completed appointments per department.
14. Retrieve all patients and the total duration of medicines prescribed to them.
15. List patients who have appointments with multiple doctors on the same day.
