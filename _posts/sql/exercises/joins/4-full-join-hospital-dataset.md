## **4️⃣ FULL OUTER JOIN Questions**

### **Basic (5)**

1. List all patients and appointments, including patients with no appointments and appointments with no patients.
2. Show all doctors and their appointments, including doctors with no appointments and appointments with no doctors.
3. List all patients and prescriptions, including patients without prescriptions and prescriptions without patients.
4. Retrieve all appointments and prescriptions, including appointments without prescriptions and prescriptions without appointments.
5. Show all doctors and prescriptions, even if a doctor has not prescribed anything or some prescriptions have missing doctor info.

### **Intermediate (5)**

6. Find all patients and doctors, including unmatched patients and doctors (patients with no doctor visits and doctors with no patients).
7. List all appointments and patients, including appointments with missing patient info and patients with no appointments.
8. Retrieve all patients and medicines prescribed, including unmatched patients and prescriptions.
9. Show all doctors and total number of prescriptions, including doctors with zero prescriptions.
10. List all appointments with patients and prescriptions, including appointments without patients or prescriptions.

### **Advanced (5)**

11. Find patients who either have no appointments or no prescriptions.
12. Retrieve doctors with appointments but no prescriptions and doctors with prescriptions but no appointments.
13. List patients with the total number of appointments and prescriptions, including unmatched records.
14. Show all doctors and their patients, including doctors who have never seen a patient and patients never seen by a doctor.
15. Find appointments where either the patient or doctor information is missing.
