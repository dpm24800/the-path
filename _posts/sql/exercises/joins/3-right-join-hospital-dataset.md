
## **3 RIGHT JOIN Questions**

### **Basic (5)**
1. List all appointments and corresponding patients, even if the patient details are missing.
2. Show all prescriptions and the corresponding appointments, even if some appointments are missing.
3. List all doctors and the appointments, even if some doctors have no appointments.
4. Retrieve all prescriptions along with doctor names, even if some prescriptions don’t have doctors.
5. List all appointments and patient details, even if patient info is missing.

### **Intermediate (5)**
6. Find all appointments and count of prescriptions per appointment (include appointments with no prescriptions).
7. List all doctors and their patients for the last month (include doctors without patients).
8. Show all prescriptions and the doctor who prescribed them (include prescriptions without doctor info).
9. Retrieve all appointments and the doctor’s specialty (include appointments with missing doctor info).
10. List patients along with all appointments and prescriptions (include prescriptions with missing appointments).

### **Advanced (5)**
11. Find all doctors who have never prescribed any medicine.
12. Retrieve appointments where prescriptions exist but patient data is missing.
13. Show all prescriptions and the total count of appointments per doctor, including prescriptions without appointments.
14. List doctors who have appointments but no medicines prescribed.
15. Retrieve patients and the number of completed appointments, including appointments with missing patient info.
