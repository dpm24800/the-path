
## **6 SELF JOIN Questions**

### **Basic (5)**
1. List patients and their previous appointment (self-join on appointments).
2. Show doctors and their colleagues in the same department.
3. List patients who share the same doctor.
4. Find appointments scheduled on the same day as other appointments.
5. Retrieve doctors who have the same specialty as another doctor.

### **Intermediate (5)**
6. Show patients and their next appointment (self-join on appointments sorted by date).
7. List doctors and other doctors who share patients with them.
8. Find patients who have multiple appointments with the same doctor.
9. Retrieve appointments with overlapping times for the same doctor.
10. List doctors who supervise other doctors in the same department (if ManagerID exists).

### **Advanced (5)**
11. Find patients who have appointments with multiple doctors on the same day.
12. Retrieve doctors who have prescribed the same medicine to multiple patients.
13. List patients who have identical appointment schedules.
14. Show doctors whose patients also have appointments with other doctors in the same department.
15. Find sequences of appointments for patients where the same doctor sees them consecutively.
