
## **2 LEFT JOIN Questions**

### **Basic (5)**
1. List all patients and their appointments (include patients with no appointments).
2. Show all doctors and the appointments they have (include doctors with no appointments).
3. Get all patients and the medicines prescribed to them (include patients with no prescriptions).
4. List all appointments and the corresponding prescriptions (include appointments without prescriptions).
5. Show all doctors and the patients they have seen (include doctors who have not seen any patient).

### **Intermediate (5)**
6. Find all patients and the count of prescriptions they have (include patients with zero prescriptions).
7. List all doctors and the number of completed appointments they have (include doctors with zero completed appointments).
8. Show patients and doctors they have appointments with, even if no medicine was prescribed.
9. Retrieve all patients and their last appointment date (include patients with no appointments).
10. List all doctors and the total number of medicines prescribed by them.

### **Advanced (5)**
11. Find patients who have not received any prescriptions.
12. List doctors who have never had a completed appointment.
13. Show all patients with the total number of medicines prescribed, including those with none.
14. Retrieve all patients along with doctors and the total number of appointments, including patients with no appointments.
15. Find doctors who have appointments but no prescriptions have been issued.
