

## **5️⃣ CROSS JOIN Questions**

### **Basic (5)**

1. List all possible combinations of patients and doctors.
2. Show all possible pairs of patients and medicines.
3. List every doctor with every appointment date.
4. Retrieve all combinations of patients and departments.
5. Show all doctors with every prescription available.

### **Intermediate (5)**

6. Find all possible patient-doctor-medicine combinations.
7. List every patient with every appointment and every doctor.
8. Show all possible combinations of doctors and specialties.
9. Retrieve all patient-doctor combinations and count how many appointments each combination has.
10. List every medicine and doctor combination for reporting purposes.

### **Advanced (5)**

11. Generate all possible combinations of patients, doctors, and appointments to identify missing data.
12. Find combinations of doctors and patients who never had an appointment.
13. List all possible combinations of prescriptions and appointments, including ones that were never prescribed.
14. Show all possible patient-doctor-department combinations.
15. Generate combinations to test which patients could see which doctors based on specialty.
